Verve

Description

Dance is a beautiful form of art that allows you to express yourself in the most vivid way possible. It often includes the depiction of a story in the form of expressions and body movements. Presenting to you, Verve, Symphony’s Group Dance Competition. We provide you an incredible platform to flaunt your dance moves and set the stage on fire! We’re in search of dance teams with utmost enthusiasm and dedication to come perform for Verve and showcase their best dance moves. Make sure you register to prove your team’s worth and win a hefty prize amount from ensemble.dance.studios!

Rules

General Rules

  1. All participating members are current college students, albeit not necessarily from the same college.
  2. Participants may use more than one song in their performance.
  3. The minimum number of participants in a team is 5.
  4. Loop dance.
  5. Obscenity/Vulgarity of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification. Songs should be chosen accordingly.
  6. Decency in dressing should be maintained at all times. The minimum knee-length costume is allowed but with compulsory stockings. No shears/tears are allowed.
  7. Usage of props is allowed(Subject to approval).
  8. Any kind of fluid, flame or heavy object is not allowed during performance.
  9. While recording,the audio should be clearly audible and video quality should be good. Ensure that the frame is angled such that expression and all body movements are clearly visible.
  10. Teams are STRICTLY prohibited from mentioning their college names during the performance by any medium.
  11. The decision of the judges and organizers shall be final and binding.
  12. Email should be sent to kjscesymphony@somaiya.edu with the subject: “Verve- Team Name” latest by 4th March,2021 .
  13. The email should have the following:
    • The music in mp3 format.
    • The video in mp4 format.
    • Team Name
    • Participants’ Name
    • Phone Numbers
    • College name(s)
    • Prop List (if any)
  14. Genre: Open
  15. Last date for registration:2nd March,2021.
